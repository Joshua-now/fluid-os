# Fluid Productions — Business Context

## Company
Fluid Productions LLC, established 2017. Now transitioning into AI-powered automation services for home service contractors. Operating lean, emphasis on execution, speed, and acquiring paying customers quickly.

## Target Market
Home service businesses — HVAC contractors, roofing contractors (highest priority), plumbing companies. These businesses run ads (Facebook, Google), generate inbound leads, and consistently lose deals because they respond too slowly, miss after-hours calls, and fail to follow up.

## Core Problem We Solve
Contractors waste ad spend because:
- They miss after-hours calls
- They respond to leads too slowly (industry avg: 47 minutes; our bots: 60 seconds)
- They fail to follow up consistently
- Every missed call is a lost job
